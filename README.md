**Ninja Alley** is a 2D local-multiplayer fighting game where two players go head-to-head on the same keyboard. Move, jump, attack, and time your skills right — the one who lands the killing blow takes the round.

This was built as my **CPE101 Engineering Exploration** project at KMUTT — a pair project I worked on with a friend.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Engine:** Unity
- **Language:** C#
- **Design tools:** Figma (UI mockups & assets)

## ✨ The part I'm most proud of

The **skill system** — players aren't just spamming attacks. Each player has two tactical abilities:

- 🛡️ **Shield** — reduce incoming damage
- ⚔️ **Damage Boost** — power up your next hit

It turns every match into little mind games: do I burn my shield now, or save it? Is my opponent about to power up? That layer of decision-making is what makes the game fun to replay.

## 🎮 Controls

| Action | Player 1 | Player 2 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Move | `A` / `D` | `←` / `→` |
| Jump | `W` | `↑` |
| Attack | `Spacebar` | `Enter` |
| Shield | `Q` | `↓` |
| Damage Boost | `E` | `Left Shift` |

This was a **pair project**, and my contributions focused on:
